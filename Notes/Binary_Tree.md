Terminology Check

Since you are re-learning these, here are three terms you'll see often for this specific shape:

    Complete Binary Tree: A tree where every level is completely filled, except possibly the last, which is filled from left to right. (This is exactly what your insert method creates!).

    Full Binary Tree: A tree where every node has either 0 or 2 children (no "single parents").

    Perfect Binary Tree: A tree where all interior nodes have two children and all leaves are at the same level.
